How to reach bhilwara?

×

By Flight

Instead of Bhilwara you can a get flight to Dabok Airport on regular basis. 
Bhilwara 134 km away Dabok Airport (UDR), Udaipur, Rajasthan
Bhilwara 199 km away Sanganeer Airport (JAI), Jaipur, Rajasthan
×

By Train

The railway station of Bhilwara is established in the heart of the city. It is well connected to other cities like Kanpur, Bhopal, Ajmer, Patna, Ahmedabad, Jaipur, Delhi, Gwalior, Lucknow and Jodhpur etc.

 Some of the trains running via Bhilwara Railway station are:
                        -Bhopal Jaipur Express 
                        -Ajmer Hyderabad Special
                        -Chetak Express
                        -Udaipur-Jaipur Intercity Express
×

By Road

The National Highway No. 79 makes Bhilwara well-connected to the most of the cities like Ajmer, Jaipur, Udaipur, etc. There are many private buses also available to reach bhilwara from various places of Rajasthan.
